# **App Name**: SwastyaScan

## Core Features:

- Image-Based Skin Disease Analysis: Allows users to upload images of skin conditions for AI-driven analysis to identify potential dermatological diseases.
- Text-Based Heart Symptom Analysis: Enables users to input text descriptions of heart-related symptoms for an AI-powered assessment of potential cardiovascular conditions.
- Disease Diagnostic Tool: Utilizes machine learning models to analyze inputs and identify the name of the potential disease and its likely causes.
- Personalized Guidance & Treatment Tool: Generates a suggested course of medicines/treatments and a routine to be followed, tailored to the identified condition.
- Encouragement Message Generator Tool: Crafts supportive and encouraging words for the user to mitigate fear and provide reassurance after receiving their analysis.
- Integrated Results Display: Presents a comprehensive output including disease name, cause, treatment, routine, and encouragement messages in a clear, easy-to-understand format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep blue (#2952A3) for a sense of trust and professionalism, offering clear contrast against the light background.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#E6EEF8) that promotes calm and cleanliness, setting a serene stage for health information.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan (#3ACBED) for interactive elements and highlights, chosen to complement the primary blue while standing out effectively.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for conveying sensitive health information clearly.
- Use minimalist, clear, and universally recognizable health and diagnostic-related icons to ensure quick comprehension and a professional appearance.
- Implement a clean, spacious layout with a clear hierarchy of information. Input sections should be distinct, and analysis results prominently displayed for easy consumption.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth loading animations for analysis processes and gentle transitions for result displays, to provide a reassuring user experience during potentially anxious moments.
